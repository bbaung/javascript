window.onload

window.onload is an event that triggers when the entire page, including all dependent resources such as stylesheets and images, has finished loading. The defer attribute, on the other hand, is used with the <script> tag to ensure that the script is executed after the document has been parsed but before the DOMContentLoaded event.
